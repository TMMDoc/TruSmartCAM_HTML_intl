Manual remnant management

Configure manual remnant

  • Click Factory → Settings → Job.

  • Select Update sheet inventory on nest (un)release.

This ensures that remnants are tracked and updated in the sheet inventory whenever a nest is completed or released.

Manual remnant sheet from job

  • Right-click on a marked complete layout (layout with green color tick).

  • Click Adds to Remnants option.

  • Now, the remnant sheet is manually added to sheet database.

Manual remnant sheet from job nest

  • Switch to JOB → Nests Page. Use Filters and select State.

  • Pick Completed to shows all completed layouts.

  • Right-Click the layout for which manual remnant need to be created and click Adds to Remnants.

Remove remnants

If a manually created remnant sheet is damaged or lost, you can remove it from the sheet inventory without deleting it directly.

  • Right-click the remnant sheet you want to remove.

  • Click Remove from Remnants…​ option.

  • The remnant sheet is removed from the sheet database.

  • The partially shaded square icon that indicates the remnant sheet has been created is also cleared when the remnant is removed.

Remnant sheets (created by Auto/Manual) are utilized with high priority during auto nest plan or interactive nest plan.
