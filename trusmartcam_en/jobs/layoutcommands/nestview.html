Layout view

  • Selecting the nests option shows a list of all nests in the currently selected job.

  • Double-click a nest to see a detailed breakdown of that nest.

  • The nest breakdown displays:

    • Parts used in the nest.

    • Detailed information such as material used, cycle time, and other metrics.

  • You can scroll through different nests and click on any part to perform the same actions available in the Parts area.

  • Right clicking on a nest will bring up Layout panel.

    • Send to Machine…​ will release the nest for production to the machines at which point the nest option will change to Recall from Machine.

    • Add Parts…​ will allow to add additional parts to the nest.

    • Adapt/Compact…​ will allow to batch change materials or machines used in the nest.

    • Change LTT…​ will allow to apply a different laser technology for the selected material and thickness in the nest.

    • Split Layouts…​ will allow to take a nest that has several repeats into a new nest.

    • Rename…​ will allow to rename the nest.
