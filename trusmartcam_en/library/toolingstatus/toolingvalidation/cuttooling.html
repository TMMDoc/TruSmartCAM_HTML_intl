Cut tooling validation

Cut tooling validation verifies that the selected cutting tool is suitable for the part geometry, material thickness, and cutting operation. It ensures that contours can be cut without tool conflicts, incomplete cuts, or violations of machine constraints.

For complete details on supported cutting tools, cutting parameters, see the TecZone documentation on 2D Cleanup and LTT.
