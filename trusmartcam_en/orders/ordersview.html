Orders view

The options on the right side of the Orders page represent different views of orders based on their processing stage. Selecting a view updates the displayed orders, allowing you to focus on specific stages of the workflow.

Ready

Selecting the Ready view displays orders that are prepared but not yet converted into jobs.

  • Shows orders that are ready for job creation.

  • Useful for identifying orders pending processing.

Active

Selecting the Active view displays orders that have been converted into jobs and are currently being processed.

  • Represents orders already moved to the Job stage.

  • Helps track orders that are in execution.

  • Useful for monitoring ongoing production.

Done

Selecting the Done view displays orders that have completed processing.

  • Shows orders whose associated jobs are finished.

  • Indicates the workflow is complete.

  • Useful for review and record-keeping.

All

Selecting the All view displays all orders regardless of their stage.

  • Provides a complete overview of all orders.

  • Useful for searching and managing across all stages.

Order status bar

The status bar shown below each order tile indicates whether the order is linked to a job. There are two types:

  • No bar - the order is not assigned to any job.

  • With bar - the order is part of a job, and the bar shows the respective job status as listed below.
